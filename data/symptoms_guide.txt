FEVER
Symptoms: Body temperature above 38 degrees C, chills, sweating, headache, muscle aches
Common causes: Viral infections like flu and cold, bacterial infections, COVID-19, dengue, malaria, typhoid
Urgency: See doctor if fever above 103F, lasts more than 3 days, or accompanied by stiff neck, rash, or difficulty breathing
Home care: Rest, drink fluids, paracetamol for fever relief, monitor temperature every 4 hours

DENGUE FEVER
Symptoms: Sudden high fever 104F, severe headache, pain behind eyes, joint and muscle pain, fatigue, nausea, skin rash
Warning signs: Severe abdominal pain, persistent vomiting, bleeding from gums or nose, blood in urine or stool
Urgency: See doctor immediately if warning signs appear. Dengue can be life-threatening.
Note: Very common in India during monsoon season July to October

MALARIA
Symptoms: Fever with chills and sweating in cycles, headache, nausea, vomiting, muscle pain
Urgency: Always see a doctor. Malaria requires prescription treatment
Common in: Rural areas, near stagnant water, monsoon season

COMMON COLD
Symptoms: Runny nose, sore throat, cough, sneezing, mild fever, congestion
Duration: Usually 7 to 10 days
Home care: Rest, warm fluids, steam inhalation, honey and ginger tea
See doctor if: Symptoms worsen after day 3, high fever, difficulty breathing

GASTROENTERITIS
Symptoms: Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, stomach cramps, fever
Home care: ORS oral rehydration solution, bland diet like rice banana and curd, avoid spicy food
See doctor if: Blood in stool, unable to keep fluids down, symptoms last more than 2 days

TYPHOID
Symptoms: Prolonged fever lasting weeks, headache, fatigue, abdominal pain, loss of appetite
Common in: Areas with poor water sanitation, very common in India
Urgency: Always requires a doctor and antibiotic treatment

HEADACHE
Types: Tension headache most common, migraine, cluster headache
Tension headache: Pressure or tightness around head, usually both sides
Migraine: Throbbing pain usually one side, sensitivity to light and sound, nausea
See doctor immediately if: Sudden severe thunderclap headache, headache with fever and stiff neck, headache after head injury

RESPIRATORY INFECTION
Symptoms: Cough, sore throat, runny nose, difficulty breathing in severe cases
See doctor if: Difficulty breathing, chest pain, high fever, coughing blood, symptoms lasting more than 10 days

URINARY TRACT INFECTION
Symptoms: Burning sensation during urination, frequent urge to urinate, cloudy or strong-smelling urine, lower abdominal pain
Urgency: See doctor within 1 to 2 days. Requires antibiotics.
See doctor immediately if: Fever, back pain, chills which may indicate kidney infection

HYPERTENSION
Normal blood pressure: Below 120 over 80 mmHg
High blood pressure: 130 over 80 or above
Symptoms: Often none, sometimes headache, dizziness, blurred vision
Urgency: If reading above 180 over 120 with symptoms it is a medical emergency

DIABETES SYMPTOMS
High blood sugar signs: Frequent urination, excessive thirst, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, blurred vision
Low blood sugar signs: Shakiness, sweating, confusion, rapid heartbeat
Urgency: Low blood sugar is an emergency. Consume sugar immediately and call doctor.

CHEST PAIN
Possible causes: Heart attack, angina, acid reflux, muscle strain, anxiety
Emergency signs: Crushing chest pain, pain spreading to arm or jaw, sweating, shortness of breath
Urgency: Any chest pain should be evaluated by a doctor. If severe call 108 immediately.

DENGUE WARNING SIGNS
Severe abdominal pain or tenderness
Persistent vomiting more than 3 times in 24 hours
Bleeding from nose or gums
Vomiting blood or blood in stool
Rapid breathing
Fatigue or restlessness
These are severe dengue warning signs. Go to hospital immediately.

DEHYDRATION
Symptoms: Dry mouth, dark urine, no urination for 8 hours, dizziness, fatigue
Common causes: Diarrhea, vomiting, fever, not drinking enough water
Treatment: ORS oral rehydration solution, coconut water, plain water
See doctor if: Cannot keep any fluids down, very dark urine, confusion

SKIN CONDITIONS
Rash with fever: Could be dengue, chickenpox, measles or allergic reaction. See doctor.
Itchy rash: Could be allergies, eczema, or fungal infection
Hives: Allergic reaction. If throat swelling or difficulty breathing call 108 immediately.

BACK PAIN
Common causes: Muscle strain, poor posture, herniated disc
Home care: Rest, hot or cold compress, gentle stretching
See doctor if: Pain radiates down leg, numbness or weakness, pain after injury, pain with fever

ANXIETY AND STRESS
Symptoms: Racing heart, shortness of breath, sweating, trembling, worry, sleep problems
Home care: Deep breathing exercises, meditation, regular exercise, adequate sleep
See doctor if: Symptoms interfere with daily life, panic attacks, thoughts of self-harm

MIGRAINE
Symptoms: Severe throbbing headache usually one side, nausea, vomiting, sensitivity to light and sound
Duration: 4 to 72 hours
Triggers: Stress, lack of sleep, certain foods, bright lights, hormonal changes
Home care: Rest in dark quiet room, cold compress, stay hydrated
See doctor if: Worst headache of your life, headache with fever and stiff neck, headache with vision or speech problems
